Frequently Asked Questions

Typically, you will need KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN), business registration proof (Udyam Registration, GST Certificate), past 6-12 months bank statements, and a detailed Project Report (DPR). For PMEGP, you also require EDP training certification and a population certificate if rural. Our advisory team assists in preparing the project report and structuring your application.

Lenders calculate loan eligibility based on your net income, credit score, and existing debt obligations. They typically allow 50% to 65% of your net monthly income to go towards loan repayments (known as FOIR). Use our **EMI Calculator** tab and switch to **Loan Eligibility** mode to estimate the maximum loan amount you can qualify for based on a comfortable monthly payment.

Net Present Value (NPV) tells you the absolute dollar/rupee amount of net wealth a project will create in today's terms. Internal Rate of Return (IRR) is the percentage yield the project produces. A project is financially viable if NPV is greater than zero and the IRR exceeds your cost of capital (hurdle rate). Use our **NPV tab** to compute both metrics simultaneously.

Project and construction finance require specialized debt structuring due to developer risk and execution milestones. To begin, submit your name and phone number in our callback form at the bottom of the page, selecting **Project Loan** or **Construction Finance**. Our advisory desk will get in touch to analyze your project layout, prepare files, and coordinate banking approvals.
